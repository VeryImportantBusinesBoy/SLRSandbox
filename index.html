/ Reflection — AI Sandbox

May 2026 · Voice Note

The SLR AI immersion program — the "Sandbox" — has been a positive experience. What works: the freedom to use tools and capabilities not available in the normal work environment; watching colleagues get hooked on it for the first time; a group of sharp people to test ideas against; and real cross-org connection. SLR is built from multiple acquisitions, and the Sandbox has been a way to actually meet people across the company.

It has also been a personal stretch. Command-line work isn't where I usually spend time — I'm normally in GitHub, not the terminal — and the Sandbox pushes into that territory.

The open question is the rollout model: a small group of tool makers building for everyone, or a ubiquitous tool on every desk like Excel. That answer shapes training, support, governance, hiring, and how the value gets measured.
